After completing a task or session, take time to reflect on the process and outcomes. Use the following framework to analyze performance and identify areas for improvement.

# Reflection Framework

1. Identify config files used
   - List all agents, skills, and commands utilized during the session.
   - Note any new tools or techniques applied.
2. Evaluate performance
   - What went well? Highlight successful strategies and actions.
   - What could be improved? Identify challenges and obstacles faced.
   - How effectively were resources utilized?
   - How long did it take to reach a solution? (prompts, time, tokens)
3. Analyze outcomes
   - Did the results meet the initial goals?
   - How long take to reach the outcome?
4. Plan for improvement
   - How can processes be optimized next time?
   - Are any new commands, agents, skills, or tools needed?
5. Document lessons learned
   - Summarize key takeaways from the session.
   - Suggest improvements to relevant config files in @~/.config/opencode
   - Be specific about changes to implement.
   - Be succinct and actionable.
